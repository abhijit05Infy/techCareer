We are looking for Veterans for our Technician Training Program

Military Veterans are now eligible to use their GI Bill benefits to attend Mercesdes-Benz DRIVE


Mercedes-Benz USA is very pleased to announce that Mercesdes-Benz DRIVE, MBUSA's 16-week Technician Training and Development Program, is now an official Apprenticeship Program through the U.S. Department of Labor and U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs. This distinction allows Military Veterans to not only receive their GI Bill benefits during the 4 month program, but also up to 8 months once employed at an Authorized Mercedes-Benz dealership.


For more information about advantages of Registered Apprenticeships please visit:
https://www.doleta.gov/OA/apprenticeship.cfm
