Mercedes-Benz DRIVE

Training the next generation of the world's best Technicians.

Mercedes-Benz DRIVE is a 16-week Technician training and development program housed in several state-of-the-art MBUSA Training Facilities across the country. The program is ideal for entry-level Automotive Technicians looking for a career with an Authorized Mercedes-Benz dealership. Upon successful completion of the program and after just six short months of dealership experience, students will become Systems Technicians - a title that can take years to earn outside of MB DRIVE.

Mercedes-Benz DRIVE is offered in three locations

Grapevine, TX

Long Beach, CA

Jacksonville, FL

Interested in applying ?

Eligibility criteria is as follows:

  • US Citizen or permanent resident with valid driver's license
  • High School Graduate or GED recipient
  • Desiring employment at an Authorized Mercedes-Benz dealership within three months of graduation from MB DRIVE with willingness to relocate to dealership of hire if required
  • Successfully pass as ASE Levels 1-6 Knowledge assessment, a background check and drug-screen

College Partnerships

Gwinnett Technical College

5150 Sugarloaf Pkwy, Lawrenceville, GA 3004

Mercesdez-Benz USA has partnered with Gwinnett Technical College to train the next generation of Technicians.
The two-year Mercedes-Benz Automotive program is offered at the Gwinnett Technical College campus in Lawrenceville GA.
Students enrolled in this program complete general Technician training courses as well as Mercedes-Benz specific courses and gain hands-on experience with Mercedes-Benz vehicles, tools and information systems.

For more information on how to apply to Gwinnett Technical College please visit: http://www.gwinnetttech.edu/enrollment/



Jefferson Community and Technical College (JCTC)

1226 Rowan St., Louisville, KY 40203

Mercedes-Benz USA has partnered with Jefferson Community and Technical
College to train the next generation of Technicians for the mid-states region.
JCTC offers two training paths that are offered in parallel, the 3-semester
program for students with little to no previous automotive training
"Mercedes-Benz Accelerate" and the 2-semester program for students with
previous general automotive training or equivalent work experience
"Mercedes-Benz Training Experienced Pathway" which both are NATEF
Accredited Training Programs.
Students enrolled in these two programs will gain hands-on experience with
donated Mercedes-Benz vehicles, equipment, and information system as well
as an internship at a Mercedes-Benz dealership that is embedded within the
program

For more information on how to apply to JCTC please visit:
https://jefferson.kctcs.edu/education-training/mercedes-benz.aspx
